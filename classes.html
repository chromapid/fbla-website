Classes

We offer various classes for kids ages 7-14. Classes last 8 weeks; see session dates below.

Each class is offered on multiple days so you can decide which day works best for your schedule.

Class Information

Class Age Group Times Offered Summary
Stars and Planets Ages 7 to 9 Mondays from 4:00 to 5:00 PM Wednesdays from 4:00 to 5:00 PM Fridays from 7:00 to 8:00 PM Students learn about the basics of astronomy, including the solar system, constellations, asteroids, and comets.
Stellar Lifecycles Ages 10 to 12 Tuesdays from 4:00 to 5:00 PM Thursdays from 4:00 to 5:00 PM Stellar Lifecycles discusses stars, white dwarfs, neutron stars, and much more.
Telescope Operation Ages 10 to 12 Fridays from 8:30 to 9:30 PM Saturdays from 8:30 to 9:30 PM Students learn how to operate a telescope, and how to find objects in the sky.
Aerospace Design Ages 8 to 10 Fridays from 4:00 to 5:00 PM Saturdays from 4:00 to 5:00 PM Saturdays from 2:00 to 3:00 PM We make paper airplanes, model parachutes, and other fun contraptions to illustrate aerodynamics!
Aerospace Design 2 Ages 10 to 12 Fridays from 5:00 to 6:00 PM Saturdays from 3:00 to 4:00 PM We create and launch air-propelled model rockets while learning and applying aerodynamics concepts.

Schedule Information
