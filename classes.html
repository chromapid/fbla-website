Classes

We offer various classes for kids ages 7-14. Classes last 8 weeks; see session dates below.

Each class is offered on multiple days so you can decide which day works best for your schedule.

Class Information

Class Age Group Times Summary
Stars and Planets Ages 7 to 9 Mondays 4:00 to 5:00 Students learn about the basics of astronomy, including the solar system, constellations, asteroids, and comets.
Stellar Lifecycles Ages 10 to 12 Tuesdays 4:00 to 5:00 Stellar Lifecycles discusses stars, white dwarfs, neutron stars, and much more.

Session Information

Classes are grouped into 8 week sessions.
